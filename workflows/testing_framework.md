# A/B Testing Framework for UGC Ads

## TESTING HIERARCHY
Test in this order — change ONE variable at a time:
1. Hook (biggest impact)
2. Script body angle
3. CTA style
4. Video length (30s vs 60s vs 90s)
5. Platform
6. Audience targeting

## WEEK-BY-WEEK TEST PLAN

### WEEK 1–2: HOOK BATTLE
Run 3 ads — identical script, different hooks
Budget: $30–50/day per variation
Declare winner when: 1 ad has 50%+ lower CPM and 30%+ higher CTR

### WEEK 3–4: CTA BATTLE
Take winning hook + script
Test: Soft CTA vs Direct CTA vs Offer CTA
Winner = lowest CPA

### WEEK 5–6: LENGTH BATTLE
Test: 30s vs 60s vs 90s (if applicable)
Look at: Watch-through %, CTR, and CPA together

### WEEK 7–8: PLATFORM BATTLE
Take best-performing full ad
Test natively on: TikTok vs Reels vs Facebook
Allocate budget to highest-ROAS platform

## KEY METRICS DASHBOARD

| Metric             | What It Tells You              | Target Benchmark     |
|--------------------|-------------------------------|----------------------|
| 3-Second Retention | Hook strength                 | > 40%                |
| 25% Watch-Through  | Intro engagement              | > 30%                |
| 100% Watch-Through | Overall script quality        | > 15%                |
| CTR (Link Click)   | CTA + offer strength          | > 1.5% (cold traffic)|
| CPC                | Audience relevance            | < $1.50              |
| CPA                | Full funnel efficiency        | < 3x product price   |
| ROAS               | Campaign profitability        | > 2.5x               |

## OPTIMIZATION DECISION TREE

3s retention < 25%? → New hook immediately
3s retention OK, but CTR low? → Rewrite CTA
CTR OK, but CPA high? → Fix landing page or offer (not the ad)
CPA OK, ROAS low? → Raise product price or add order bump
Everything OK? → Scale by 20% budget every 48 hours

## CREATIVE FATIGUE SIGNALS
- CTR dropping >30% week-over-week
- CPM rising >50% from baseline
- Frequency > 3.5 on cold audiences

Action: Refresh hook + CTA, keep winning script body
