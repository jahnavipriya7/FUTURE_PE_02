# 🧪 TESTING FRAMEWORK — Measure, Learn, Scale

> **Data-driven decisions for your UGC ad performance.**

---

## TESTING PHILOSOPHY

> "Never run one ad. Always run experiments."

The goal of testing is to isolate variables, measure their impact, and systematically improve performance. One winning creative insight can 10x your ROAS.

---

## WHAT TO TEST (AND IN WHAT ORDER)

### Priority Hierarchy

```
1st → HOOK          (biggest impact on cost per result)
2nd → CREATIVE      (visual style, creator type)
3rd → OFFER         (% discount vs. $ off vs. free shipping)
4th → CTA           (soft vs. hard vs. community)
5th → CAPTION       (long vs. short, story vs. list)
```

**Rule:** Only test ONE variable at a time. Everything else stays identical.

---

## HOOK TESTING PROTOCOL

### Setup
- Budget: $5–$10/day per hook variation
- Duration: 3–5 days minimum (allow algorithm learning)
- Audience: Same audience across all variants
- Creatives: Identical from 3 seconds onward — only the hook differs

### Decision Criteria

| Metric | Cut Below | Keep Above | Scale Above |
|--------|-----------|------------|-------------|
| Thumb Stop Rate | <15% | 20–25% | >30% |
| 3-Second View Rate | <20% | 25–35% | >40% |
| Watch-Through Rate | <30% | 40–50% | >60% |
| CTR (link click) | <0.8% | 1–1.5% | >2% |

### Week-by-Week Schedule

**Week 1:** Launch 3–5 hook variants at equal budget  
**Week 2:** Kill bottom 60% (lowest thumb stop + CTR). Double budget on top 2.  
**Week 3:** Declare winner. Create 3 variations of the winner (new hook, same structure).  
**Week 4+:** Scale winner. Refresh with new variant every 3–4 weeks.

---

## CREATIVE TESTING PROTOCOL

After finding a winning hook, test:

### Variable A — Creator Type
- Creator 1: Relatable everyday person (same target demo)
- Creator 2: Expert/authority figure
- Creator 3: Aspirational personality

**KPI:** Cost per Purchase (who converts best, not just clicks)

### Variable B — Visual Style
- Style 1: Talking head only
- Style 2: Talking head + B-roll
- Style 3: Mostly B-roll, voiceover only

**KPI:** Watch-through rate + CTR

### Variable C — Energy Level
- High energy: Fast cuts, expressive delivery, upbeat audio
- Low energy: Calm, conversational, minimal music

**KPI:** Cost per Lead / Cost per Purchase (depends on your goal)

---

## OFFER TESTING PROTOCOL

**Never run an offer you haven't tested.** Even a 5% difference in offer structure can double conversion rate.

Test these offer formats:
- `"20% off" vs "$10 off"` — (percentage vs. dollar amount)
- `"Free shipping"` vs `"Buy 2 get 1 free"` vs `"Limited time only"`
- `"Try risk-free for 30 days"` vs `"Money-back guarantee"`

**KPI:** Conversion rate and Average Order Value (AOV)

---

## METRICS REFERENCE CARD

### Awareness-Stage Metrics (Top of Funnel)
| Metric | Definition | Good Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| Thumb Stop Rate | % who stopped scrolling | >25% |
| 3-Second View Rate | % who watched 3+ seconds | >30% |
| Video Completion Rate | % who watched to end | >30% (15s), >20% (30s) |

### Consideration Metrics (Mid-Funnel)
| Metric | Definition | Good Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| CTR | % who clicked link | >1.0% |
| Engaged CPM | Cost per 1000 engaged | Varies by niche |
| Save Rate | % who saved the post | >2% |
| Comment Rate | % who commented | >1% |

### Conversion Metrics (Bottom of Funnel)
| Metric | Definition | Good Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| CPC | Cost per link click | <$1.00 (varies) |
| CPL | Cost per lead | Varies by niche |
| CPP | Cost per purchase | Must be < AOV/3 |
| ROAS | Revenue ÷ Ad Spend | >2x (scaling), >3x (profitable) |

## SCALE DECISION FRAMEWORK

```
Thumb Stop >25% AND CTR >1% AND CPP < target?
→ SCALE: Increase budget 20–30% every 2–3 days

Thumb Stop >25% but CTR <1%?
→ PROBLEM: Script middle or CTA. Keep hook, rewrite end.

Thumb Stop <20%?
→ PROBLEM: Hook. Kill this. Test a new hook.

CTR >1.5% but low conversions?
→ PROBLEM: Landing page or offer, not the ad.
```
