# Testing Framework

## A/B Testing Ideas
- Hook vs Hook
- Soft CTA vs Direct CTA
- 60 sec vs 90 sec
- Trending audio vs neutral audio

## Metrics To Track
- 3-second retention
- Watch-through rate
- CTR
- CPC
- CPA
- ROAS

## Optimization Logic

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Low retention | Change hook |
| Low CTR | Change CTA |
| Low conversion | Improve social proof |
| High CPC | Test new creative angle |
| Low watch-through | Shorten the script |

## Testing Schedule
- Week 1: Launch 3–5 hook variants at equal budget
- Week 2: Kill bottom 50%, double budget on top performers
- Week 3: Declare winning hook, create 3 variations of it
- Week 4+: Scale winner. Refresh creative every 4–6 weeks

## Benchmarks
| Metric | Good | Great |
|--------|------|-------|
| 3-second retention | >25% | >40% |
| Watch-through | >30% | >55% |
| CTR | >1% | >2% |
| ROAS | >2x | >3.5x |
