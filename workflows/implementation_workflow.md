# Implementation Workflow

## STEP 1: Gather product info
Open `/templates/product_input_template.md` and fill in all fields completely.

## STEP 2: Generate hooks
Use `/prompts/02_hook_generator.md` — paste product info and generate 15 hooks.

## STEP 3: Select top 3 hooks
Pick the 3 strongest hooks across different emotional angles for testing.

## STEP 4: Build scripts
Use `/prompts/03_problem_solution_script.md` — build full scripts for each selected hook.

## STEP 5: Adapt for platform
Use `/prompts/04_platform_adaptation.md` — adapt your best script per platform.

## STEP 6: Customize brand voice
Use `/prompts/05_brand_voice_customization.md` — apply your brand tone and language.

## STEP 7: Add psychology triggers
Use `/prompts/07_conversion_psychology.md` — layer in conversion triggers.

## STEP 8: Launch & test
Film, edit, and publish. Set up A/B tests for your 3 hooks.

## STEP 9: Optimize based on metrics
Use `/workflows/testing_framework.md` to analyze results and iterate.
