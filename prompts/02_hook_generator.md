# Hook Generator

## Purpose
Generate 15 scroll-stopping hooks using psychology frameworks.

## Best For
Top-of-funnel campaigns.

## Instructions
1. Add product name and audience.
2. Generate hooks.
3. Test top 3 hooks.

## Prompt

```
You are a UGC hook specialist. Generate 15 scroll-stopping hooks for:

Product: [YOUR PRODUCT NAME]
Target Audience: [WHO IS THIS FOR]
Core Pain Point: [MAIN PROBLEM]
Core Desire: [WHAT THEY WANT]
Transformation: [BEFORE → AFTER]

Use these hook formulas:
1. Pattern Interrupt — "Wait — [unexpected claim]"
2. Curiosity Gap — "The #1 reason [audience] can't [outcome] is NOT what you think"
3. Bold Claim — "I [achieved result] in [timeframe] without [sacrifice]"
4. POV — "POV: You finally found [solution]"
5. Negative Contrast — "Why does [category] never work? Until this."
6. Direct Call-Out — "If you're [audience], this will change everything"
7. Social Proof Tease — "[Number] people use this and no one's talking about it"
8. Loss Aversion — "You're losing [money/time] every day you don't know about this"
9. Transformation Story — "6 months ago I was [before]. Now [after]."
10. Myth Bust — "Everything you know about [topic] is wrong."

For each hook include:
- Hook line
- Why it works (1 sentence)
- Suggested opening visual
```
