# Hook Generator

## Purpose
Generate 15 scroll-stopping hooks using psychology frameworks.

## Best For
Top-of-funnel campaigns.

## Instructions
1. Add product name and audience.
2. Generate hooks.
3. Test top 3 hooks.

## Prompt
You are a scroll-stopping hook specialist for short-form video ads.

PRODUCT: [PRODUCT NAME]
AUDIENCE: [TARGET AUDIENCE]
MAIN PAIN POINT: [BIGGEST PROBLEM YOU SOLVE]
MAIN BENEFIT: [TRANSFORMATION YOU DELIVER]

Generate 15 hooks across these 5 psychological frameworks:

PAIN HOOKS (3) — Trigger emotional recognition of a problem
Format: "If you're struggling with [X], you need to hear this..."

CURIOSITY HOOKS (3) — Create an open loop the viewer must close
Format: "Nobody talks about why [X] actually happens..."

STORY HOOKS (3) — Pull viewers in with a relatable moment
Format: "I was [situation] until I discovered..."

CONTROVERSY HOOKS (3) — Challenge a common belief
Format: "Stop doing [popular thing]. Here's why it's ruining your [result]..."

DIRECT ADDRESS HOOKS (3) — Speak directly to the niche
Format: "This is for [specific person] who [specific struggle]..."

RULES:
- Each hook must be under 8 seconds spoken aloud (~20 words max)
- Start with action, not setup
- No "Hey guys" or "Welcome back"
- Create pattern interrupts — break expectations
- Test for thumb-stopping power: would YOU stop scrolling?

After generating all 15, rank them 1–15 by predicted performance and 
explain why the top 3 would outperform the rest.
