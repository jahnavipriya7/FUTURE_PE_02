# 🪝 HOOK GENERATOR — 10 Proven Formulas for Scroll-Stopping Openers

> **The hook is the most important part of your ad. You have 1.5 seconds.**

---

## THE PROMPT

```
You are a UGC hook specialist. Your only job is to write attention-grabbing opening lines for short-form video ads.

Product: [YOUR PRODUCT NAME]
Target Audience: [WHO IS THIS FOR]
Core Pain Point: [MAIN PROBLEM]
Core Desire: [WHAT THEY WANT]
Transformation: [BEFORE → AFTER]

Generate 10 hooks using each of the following formulas:

FORMULA 1 — PATTERN INTERRUPT
"Wait — [unexpected claim or observation]"

FORMULA 2 — CURIOSITY GAP
"The #1 reason [audience] can't [desired outcome] is NOT what you think"

FORMULA 3 — BOLD CLAIM
"I [achieved result] in [timeframe] without [common sacrifice]"

FORMULA 4 — RELATABLE SCENARIO (POV)
"POV: You finally found [solution to problem]"

FORMULA 5 — NEGATIVE CONTRAST
"Why does [product category] never actually work? Until this."

FORMULA 6 — DIRECT CALL-OUT
"If you're [specific audience identity], this will change everything"

FORMULA 7 — SOCIAL PROOF TEASE
"[Number] people are using this to [outcome] — and no one's talking about it"

FORMULA 8 — LOSS AVERSION
"You're losing [money/time/opportunity] every day you don't know about this"

FORMULA 9 — TRANSFORMATION STORY
"6 months ago I was [before state]. Now [after state]. Here's what changed."

FORMULA 10 — CONTROVERSY/MYTH BUST
"Everything you know about [topic] is wrong. Here's the truth."

For each hook:
- Write the hook line
- Write a 1-sentence "why it works" explanation
- Suggest a visual/action to pair with it
```

---

## 10 HOOK FORMULAS AT A GLANCE

| # | Formula | Best For |
|---|---------|----------|
| 1 | Pattern Interrupt | Any niche, cold traffic |
| 2 | Curiosity Gap | Educational / SaaS |
| 3 | Bold Claim | Fitness, beauty, finance |
| 4 | POV | Lifestyle, D2C |
| 5 | Negative Contrast | Frustrated audiences |
| 6 | Direct Call-Out | Niche audiences |
| 7 | Social Proof Tease | New products with traction |
| 8 | Loss Aversion | High-ticket / productivity |
| 9 | Transformation Story | Before/after products |
| 10 | Myth Bust | Saturated markets |

---

## ⚡ POWER TIPS

1. **Never start with your brand name.** Nobody cares yet.
2. **Use "you" or "your" in the first 5 words** to create instant relevance.
3. **Numbers outperform vague language.** "Lost 12 lbs" beats "lost weight."
4. **Open loops work.** Promise to reveal something and delay the payoff.
5. **Test 3 hooks minimum** before scaling any ad.
