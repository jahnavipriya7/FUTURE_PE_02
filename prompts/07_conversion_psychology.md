## PSYCHOLOGY TRIGGER REFERENCE GUIDE

### The Big 7 Triggers for UGC Ads

| Trigger | How to Use It | Example Line |
|---------|--------------|--------------|
| **Urgency** | Time deadline | "Offer ends tonight at midnight" |
| **Scarcity** | Quantity limit | "We only made 500 units" |
| **Social Proof** | Numbers + specifics | "82,000 five-star reviews" |
| **Authority** | Credibility signal | "Recommended by 9/10 dermatologists" |
| **Loss Aversion** | Cost of inaction | "You're already paying for the problem" |
| **Identity** | Self-concept alignment | "For women who don't do compromise" |
| **Transformation** | Vivid before/after | "From hiding in hoodies to loving mirrors" |

### Trigger Stacking by Funnel Stage

**Cold Traffic (Awareness):**
- Lead with: Identity + Curiosity + Novelty
- Close with: Social Proof + Soft CTA

**Warm Traffic (Consideration):**
- Lead with: Social Proof + Authority
- Close with: Urgency + Loss Aversion + Hard CTA

**Retargeting (Decision):**
- Lead with: Scarcity + Urgency
- Close with: Risk reversal ("30-day guarantee") + Hard CTA

---

## ETHICAL GUIDELINES

✅ Only use urgency/scarcity if it's **real**  
✅ Social proof numbers must be **accurate**  
✅ Transformation claims must be **achievable and honest**  
✅ Never manufacture false authority  
✅ Aim for persuasion that creates **genuine value**, not manipulation
