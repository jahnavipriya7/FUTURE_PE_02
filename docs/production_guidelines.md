# Production Guidelines

## Lighting
Use natural light whenever possible.
Face a window — never have light behind you.
If indoors without natural light, use a ring light or softbox at eye level.
Good lighting is more important than an expensive camera.

## Camera
Phone filming preferred for UGC authenticity.
iPhone 12+ or Android flagship in portrait mode (9:16).
Film at eye level or slightly above — never below chin.
Use the rear camera for better quality when you can angle it.

## Editing
Keep natural pauses. Avoid over-polishing.
Use CapCut or DaVinci Resolve for free editing.
Auto-captions: always review for accuracy before publishing.
Add text overlays — 85% of content is watched on mute.

## Framing
Vertical 9:16 format always.
Leave headroom: eyes in the upper third of frame.
Background: clean, uncluttered, or lifestyle-relevant.
No visible logos from competing brands.

## Authenticity Tips
- Speak conversationally — not like you're reading a script
- Show real product usage on screen
- Avoid scripted, stiff delivery — do more takes until it feels natural
- Include imperfections — they build trust in UGC content

## Export Settings
Resolution: 1080 x 1920 (9:16)
Frame rate: 30fps minimum
Format: MP4 (H.264)
Audio: AAC, clear voiceover at 0dB, music at -12dB

## Hook Filming Rule
Film your hook last — after you've warmed up.
Film it 10+ times. Get 3 completely different deliveries.
The hook is worth more production time than the rest of the video combined.
