## LOCAL BUSINESS SPECIFIC TIPS

### Geo-Targeting is Everything
- Always mention the city/neighborhood in the first 5 seconds
- Use location-specific hashtags
- Run ads targeted to a 5–15km radius of the business

### Trust Signals That Work for Local
- Show the actual premises (clean, professional, welcoming)
- Feature real reviews (screenshot with name and rating)
- Show the team's faces — familiarity builds trust

### CTA for Local Business
- "Call now" or "Book online" (specific and easy)
- WhatsApp link if available
- "First appointment free" removes all friction
