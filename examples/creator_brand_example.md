## CREATOR BRAND SPECIFIC STRATEGIES

### Why Creator UGC Ads Are Different
- **YOU are the product** — your personality, credibility, and story sell the course
- Authenticity > polish — raw, honest content outperforms produced ads
- Proof beats promises — student results are your most powerful asset

### Trust Elements That Convert for Courses
1. **Your own transformation story** — before/after of your journey
2. **Student testimonials** — video > screenshot > text
3. **Proof of activity** — show live community, Q&A sessions, real engagement
4. **Specificity** — "60 days" beats "fast", "15,000 followers" beats "grew a lot"

### Urgency Framework for Course Launches
| Tactic | When to Use |
|--------|------------|
| Founding member pricing | Launch week |
| Bonuses expiring | Mid-launch |
| Enrollment closing | Last 48 hours |
| Cohort size limit | Premium/live products |
