# Creator Brand Example

## Input
Product: The Content Creator Accelerator (Online Course)
Audience: Aspiring creators aged 18–30 wanting to grow on TikTok/Instagram
Benefit: 0 to 10,000 followers in 90 days using a proven content system

## Selected Hooks
1. "I was posting every day for 6 months. Zero growth. I almost quit."
2. "My student went from 300 to 15,000 followers in 60 days. Here's what she did differently."

## Final Script

```
[0:00–0:05]
[VISUAL] Creator's old content vs new — follower count jump on screen
[VOICEOVER] "A year ago I was posting daily and getting 12 views per video. I had no idea what I was doing wrong."
[TEXT] 12 views per video after months of daily posting 😩

[0:05–0:18]
[VISUAL] Screen recording — content planning, hook writing, analytics
[VOICEOVER] "Once I figured out the actual system — the hook formula, the content calendar, the editing shortcuts — everything changed. 6 months later: 180,000 followers. I've now taught this to 1,200 students. One hit 15K in 60 days from scratch."
[TEXT] 180K followers | 1,200+ students | Results right now 📈

[0:18–0:35]
[VISUAL] Quick preview of course modules
[VOICEOVER] "The Content Creator Accelerator is my step-by-step system. No fluff. No theory. Just what actually works — even if you're starting from zero."
[TEXT] ✅ Hook Formula | ✅ Content Calendar | ✅ Growth Strategy

[0:35–0:45]
[VISUAL] Creator direct to camera, urgent tone
[VOICEOVER] "Founding member price closes in 48 hours. After that the price doubles. Link in bio."
[TEXT] ⏰ 48 hours left | ₹4,999 → ₹9,999 | Link in bio NOW
```

## Platform Adaptation

**TikTok Version:**
- Raw, handheld, conversational delivery
- Use trending audio underneath voiceover
- Caption: "6 months of daily posting = 12 views 😭 then THIS happened"

**Instagram Reels Version:**
- Slightly more polished, show before/after follower screenshots
- Caption: "From 0 to 180K. Here's the exact system (link in bio to learn it)"

## Expected Performance
High-performing with aspiring creator audiences on TikTok.
Target: >35% thumb stop, >2% CTR, CPP under ₹1,200.
