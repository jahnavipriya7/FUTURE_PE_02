# 📋 PRODUCT INPUT TEMPLATE

> **Fill this in first. This is the fuel for all your AI prompts.**

---

## INSTRUCTIONS

Complete every field. The more specific you are, the better the AI output will be.  
Vague input = generic script. Specific input = high-converting script.

After completing, copy the filled template and paste it into any prompt in `prompts/`.

## PRODUCT INFORMATION

```
PRODUCT NAME: 
[e.g., GlowRush Vitamin C Serum]

PRODUCT CATEGORY:
[e.g., skincare / fitness supplement / SaaS tool / food & beverage / apparel]

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION (1–2 sentences):
[e.g., A fast-absorbing Vitamin C serum that brightens skin and reduces dark spots in 3 weeks.]

PRICE POINT:
[e.g., $49 / $29/month / Free with premium at $15/month]

CURRENT OFFER / CTA:
[e.g., 30% off with code GLOW30 — offer ends Sunday / Free 14-day trial, no credit card]

PRODUCT URL / WHERE TO BUY:
[e.g., yoursite.com/product — link in bio]

PRODUCT LAUNCHED:
[e.g., New this month / Established, 2+ years old]
```

## BENEFITS & FEATURES

```
KEY BENEFIT #1 (Primary — what they'll talk about most):
[e.g., Reduces dark spots visibly in 21 days]

KEY BENEFIT #2 (Secondary):
[e.g., No fragrance, safe for sensitive skin]

KEY BENEFIT #3 (Tertiary):
[e.g., Cruelty-free, vegan, sustainable packaging]

UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION (what competitors don't offer):
[e.g., Only serum with 20% Vitamin C + hyaluronic acid in a single formula]

INGREDIENTS / KEY SPECS (if relevant):
[e.g., 20% L-ascorbic acid, hyaluronic acid, niacinamide]
```

## TARGET AUDIENCE

```
PRIMARY DEMOGRAPHIC:
Age range: [e.g., 24–38]
Gender: [e.g., Women / Men / Any]
Location: [e.g., US, UK, AUS / India / Global]
Income level: [e.g., Middle income / Aspirational / Premium]

PSYCHOGRAPHIC PROFILE:
Lifestyle: [e.g., Busy working professional, health-conscious, beauty enthusiast]
Values: [e.g., Clean beauty, sustainability, efficiency, results]
Content they consume: [e.g., Skincare TikTok, wellness YouTube, Instagram beauty influencers]

PERSONA NAME & STORY:
[Give your ideal customer a name and 2-sentence story]
[e.g., "Priya, 29, is a software developer who wants clear skin without spending hours on a routine. She's tried 5 different serums and nothing has worked."]
```

## PAIN POINTS & PSYCHOLOGY

```
PRIMARY PAIN POINT (stated in their language, not marketing language):
[e.g., "I've spent so much money on serums that promise to brighten my skin and none of them actually work"]

SECONDARY PAIN POINT:
[e.g., "I'm scared of harsh chemicals irritating my sensitive skin"]

EMOTIONAL TRIGGER (what they FEEL about this problem):
[e.g., Frustrated, embarrassed, hopeless, skeptical]

FAILED ALTERNATIVES (what they've already tried):
[e.g., Other high-street serums, DIY treatments, more expensive clinic treatments]

DESIRED TRANSFORMATION:
Before state: [e.g., "Dull, uneven skin tone, hiding behind filters and heavy foundation"]
After state: [e.g., "Glowing, even skin — confident going completely bare-faced"]
```
